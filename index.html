Welcome!

Hey everyone, please feel free to post resources that you find helpful in the OneDrive link below. Once reviewed and approved, they will be moved to the official OneDrive directory.

Please do not upload files not related to school subjects, doing so will result in the removal of access from this section. Below is a list of rules you must follow:

  • Only upload files related to school subjects
  • Do not upload assignments/exams (applies to both task sheet and completed work)
  • Be sure you clearly label the file you have uploaded, and make sure the filename include:
    • Year level
    • Subject
    • Unit (which topic)
    • Description (basic information about the file)
  • Do not delete files uploaded by others (we can see who deleted the file)
  • Do not upload copyrighted content (textbooks from teachers are fine)
